Who I Am

Hello! My name is Maason Kao, and I am currently a Senior at Michigan State University. I am studying Media and Information and minoring in Information Technology. Since coming to Michigan State, I have worked a variety of jobs, most notably at MSU's IT Helpdesk in the computer center. I learnt a lot about working with hardware, software, and customer service. I met a lot of great people, and really enjoyed my time there. I am currently working at MSU's Infrastructure Planning & Facilities in the IT department. The position is more focused on desktop support. The job has taught me a lot about time management, communication, and working as a team.

My goal for the summer of 2016 is to get an internship or a part-time job. With the remaining time, I aim to get my CompTIA A+ certification done. If there is time, I will also go for Network+, Security+, and Microsoft Office Suite Certification.
